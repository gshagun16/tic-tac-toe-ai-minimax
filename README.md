## 🧩 How the AI Works

The AI uses the **Minimax algorithm**, a recursive decision-making algorithm.

### 🔹 Logic:
- AI tries to **maximize** the score  
- Player tries to **minimize** the score  

### 🔹 Scoring:
- AI win → +10  
- Player win → -10  
- Draw → 0  

👉 The AI simulates all possible moves and chooses the best outcome.

---

## 🌳 Minimax Intuition

The algorithm explores a decision tree:

- AI (Maximizing player)
- Human (Minimizing player)

Each move is evaluated recursively until:
- A win is found  
- A draw occurs  

👉 This guarantees the AI never loses.
